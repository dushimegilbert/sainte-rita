Education Is The Foundation Of Culture And Science

VISION

Sainte Rita School aims for the development of each student through the free expression of their thoughts and the development of self-confidence, with respect for themselves and others; open-mindedness to the world and creativity, while maintaining a critical mind and a sense of responsibility, as a young citizen.

MISSION

The school's mission is to provide students with French and English education by promoting active and participatory methods to enable students to acquire skills and develop their autonomy to play an active and creative role in their social and professional lives in the future.

CORE VALUES

  • Active Participation
  • Creativity
  • Excellence
  • Discipline
  • Respect
  • Patriotism
  • Solidarity
  • Unity
  • Integrity
  • Self-Confidence
