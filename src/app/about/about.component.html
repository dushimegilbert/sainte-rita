Background of the School

Ecole Sainte Rita: A Legacy of Vision and Education

Ecole Sainte Rita is a school located in Gasabo district, Bumbogo Sector, a few meters ahead of AZAM. It has Nursery and Primary sections. It started with 25 students in 2018 and now has 350 students in both sections with a school motto: “Education est la fondation de la culture et de la science.”

Ecole Sainte Rita stands as a testament to the enduring power of dedicated educators and a commitment to holistic student development. Founded by a collective of ten passionate teachers known as “Teachers’ Vision,” the school's genesis lies in a shared belief in the transformative potential of education and a desire to create a nurturing and stimulating learning environment.

Origin of Teachers’ Vision

The foundation of Ecole Sainte Rita can be traced back to the collaborative efforts of these ten teachers, “Teachers’ Vision,” a group of educators united by a common ethos, and advised by Spiritual Father Didier (Regina Pacis Remera).
  • Academic Excellence: A commitment to providing a rigorous and challenging curriculum designed to cultivate critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a thirst for knowledge.
  • Holistic Development: A recognition of the importance of nurturing the whole child intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically.
  • Nurturing Environment: A dedication to creating a safe, supportive, and inclusive atmosphere where students feel valued, respected, and encouraged to reach their full potential.
  • Community Engagement: A belief in the power of collaboration and a commitment to fostering strong relationships between the school, parents, and the wider community.
  • Shared Vision: The teachers envisioned a school where innovation flourished, where students were empowered to become responsible and engaged citizens, and where the pursuit of knowledge was a shared journey.

The Founding and Early Years


Driven by their vision and collective expertise, "Teachers’ Vision" embarked on the ambitious journey of establishing Ecole Sainte Rita. The early years were marked by:

Overcoming Challenges: The founders faced the typical challenges associated with establishing a new school, including securing funding, finding suitable premises, and attracting students.

Establishing a Distinctive Identity: They worked tirelessly to create a unique school culture that reflected their commitment to excellence, student well-being, and community engagement.

Growth and Development


Over time, Ecole Sainte Rita grew and flourished, building a strong reputation for:

Academic Achievements: The school consistently demonstrated a commitment to academic excellence, with students achieving outstanding results in standardized tests and other assessments.

Innovative Programs: "Teachers’ Vision" continually sought ways to enhance the learning experience through innovative programs, extracurricular activities, and the integration of technology.

Community effects : The school became a vital part of the local community, organizing events, supporting local initiatives, and fostering a strong sense of belonging among students, parents, and staff.

Legacy and Future


Today, Ecole Sainte Rita stands as a legacy of the pioneering spirit of "Teachers’ Vision." The school continues to uphold the founding principles while adapting to the evolving needs of the 21st-century learner. The legacy of the founders continues to inspire current teachers and staff, ensuring that Ecole Sainte Rita remains a place where students are empowered to succeed academically, develop a strong moral compass, and contribute positively to the world. The school is poised to continue its journey of providing exceptional education and shaping future generations.

History of Sainte Rita of Cascia

St. RITA PATRONNE SAINTE OF THE IMPOSSIBLE


Sainte Rita of Cascia, born on May 22, 1381, in Roccaporena, Italy, was an Italian widow and Augustinian nun known for her deep faith and devotion. Forced into marriage with a violent husband, she influenced him to change, but he was later murdered. Choosing forgiveness, she encouraged her sons to do the same. After their deaths, she joined the community in Cascia, dedicating her life to prayer and service. She received a mystical wound symbolizing Christ’s suffering. She died in 1457 and was canonized in 1900, becoming the Patronne Sainte of impossible causes and reconciliation.


The reason we named our school Ecole Sainte Rita is to honor Sainte Rita of Cascia, a symbol of faith, perseverance, and resilience. Sainte Rita is widely known as the Patronne Sainte of impossible causes, reconciliation, and peace, values that we strive to instill in our students. By bearing her name, our school reflects her spirit of forgiveness, dedication, and unwavering faith, encouraging students to face challenges with courage and determination. Just as Sainte Rita overcame obstacles in her life through prayer, wisdom, and compassion, we aim to provide an education that nurtures not only academic excellence but also strong moral character, leadership, and a commitment to service. At Ecole Sainte Rita, we believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also guided by principles of integrity, kindness, and perseverance just as Sainte Rita exemplified throughout her life.


School Life

Academics

At Sainte Rita, education is structured to provide students with a balanced and effective learning experience. The school follows a well-planned schedule that ensures academic excellence while incorporating essential breaks and revision sessions to enhance learning outcomes.

Nursery Section

  • Start Time: 8:30 AM
  • Breakfast Break: 10:00 AM
  • End of Classes: 12:30 PM

Primary Section

  • Start Time: 8:30 AM
  • Breakfast Break: 10:00 AM
  • Lunch Break: 12:30 PM – 2:00 PM
  • Afternoon Session: 2:00 PM – 4:30 PM

Special Revision for Upper Primary

To prepare students for academic success, especially in the final years of primary education, Sainte Rita organizes special revision sessions for Primary Five and Primary Six every Saturday. These sessions provide additional support, ensuring that students reinforce their knowledge and excel in their studies.
By maintaining this well-structured academic routine, Sainte Rita fosters an environment where students can thrive academically while maintaining a healthy balance between learning and personal development.
